Payment Schedule

Monthly Salary

Calculation Term:
Starts from the 16th of the previous month up to 15th day of the current month
Pay Day:
Every 25th of the month (or the earliest business day If the selected payday falls on a weekend / bank holiday, payment is usually made on the first day before the holiday.

Semi-Annual Bonus

Calculation Terms:
From 16th March to 15th September/From 16th September to 15th March
Pay Day:
First Friday of July and December

Overtime Allowance

Allowances for overtime work shall be calculated as below:
Base Salary/Average Business Hours of a Month*1/4*Payout Rate

Holiday Work Allowance

1) Non-Legal Holiday (Saturdays, Public Holidays etc)

2) Legal Holiday

Holiday work wage supplement is calculated as below:
Base Salary/Average Business Hours of a Month x 45 % x 8hours
This rate is applied when an employee does not take compensatory holiday for their holiday work.
