EXTENSION OF PERIOD OF STAY

Procedures

1.Complete the Application for Extension of Period of Stay for applicant parts and the supplementary documents that corresponds with your desired residence status.
Kindly download the application form using the link below.
Application form for extension of period of stay | Immigration Services Agency of Japan (isa.go.jp)

　Intra-Company Transferee (from the link select # 6)
　Engineer/Specialist in Humanities/Int’l Services (select # 8)

2.Submit the application form first to HRD address to analyn@makino.co.jp for the checking and for other necessary details on company side, if there is no corrections HRD will forward the application form address to you (designated Department) via Internal Courier (Shanai Bin) with the Company seal required in the application.
Please make sure that your documents are complete for you to avoid inconveniences.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:

Application Form (fill -up the application form first and submit to HR for checking)

Passport

Residence Card

1 Photo (4cmx3cm) taken within 3 months

Company’s Quarterly journal (stock market share copy) (HR will include this together with the application form

3.Submit the above documents to the Immigration Bureau near you.

Location & Direction

Living in Kanagawa Prefecture

Yokohama Immigration Office
Address : 10-7 Torihama-cho, Kanazawa-ku, Yokohama Kanagawa 236-0002
Telephone : 045-769-1720

　Direction:

From Ebina station　→　(Sotetsu Line)Yokohama Station　→　(change train to Platform 3 (blue line, JR Negishi Line) take the train heading to Ofuna) get-off at Shinsugita Station.

Bus Stop in front of Immigration Office.

From Shinsugita Station head straight, notice the sign which lead you to the bus station going to the Yokohama Immigration Bureau. (Bus line 61)
